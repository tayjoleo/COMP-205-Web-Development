Our mission is simple: making the best darn cookies you've ever tasted.

What sets our cookies apart? They are objectively delicious.

How? Shhh, it’s a secret.

What we can tell you is that we use top-notch ingredients mixed with a whole lot of passion to create cookies that are soft, chewy, and packed with flavour.

But here's the real deal – our cookies are known for putting smiles on faces. Whether you're treating yourself or sharing with friends, Dough Boy's cookies are engineered to make all of life’s moments a little sweeter.

If you don’t believe us, we get it. Ultimately it just means more cookies for us.

We have all of the classics you could ever want and are constantly trying new combinations to see if we can find the next great cookie. We won’t stop until we accomplish our goal and find the long lost sister to the Chocolate Chip cookie. We are always looking for suggestions so if you have a flavour combo that you think we should try, let us know and you might just end up with your name on one of Dough Boy’s cookies.
